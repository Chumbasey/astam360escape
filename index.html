Our Story

A Sanctuary Above
the Clouds

Nestled in the ancient village of Astam, Astam 360 Escape offers a breathtaking window into the real Nepal. From here you can see the majestic Annapurna Mountain Range, the rolling hills of Ghachowk, Lwang, Ghalel, and the famous landmarks of Lahachowk, Hemja Kot, Kaun Danda — all visible from a single vantage point.

Gaze down at the shimmering Pokhara city and valley below, watch clouds drift between the hills of Ghachowk and Lwang, and wake up each morning to one of the most spectacular panoramas in all of Nepal — not just mountains, but an entire living landscape of hills, villages and valleys.

Our friendly staff, nature walks, morning yoga, and home-cooked Nepali meals make this more than a stay — it's a transformation. Enjoy breathtaking views from our dining room on the upper floor, where the Annapurnas and Pokhara valley become your backdrop for every single meal.

What We Offer

Facilities & Experiences

Annapurna Range

Marvel at the iconic Annapurna Mountain Range up close. One of Nepal's most spectacular Himalayan views, visible every clear morning from Astam.

Scenic Dining Room

Our dining room on the upper floor overlooks the Annapurna range and Pokhara valley. Every meal becomes an unforgettable experience.

Yoga & Meditation

Our peaceful surroundings and open spaces are perfect for guests who enjoy yoga or meditation. Practice freely in the lap of nature with the Himalayas as your backdrop.

Organic Garden Food

All our food is freshly grown in our own garden — 100% organic, naturally healthy and incredibly tasty. From garden to your plate! 🌱

Village Tour

Explore Astam village and experience the warm culture and lifestyle of the local Adhikari community. Available on special request only.

Pokhara Valley View

Gaze down at Pokhara city, Lahachowk, Hemja Kot, Kaun Danda and the entire valley — a breathtaking living panorama stretching below.

Friendly Staff

Our warm, welcoming team is here to make your stay as comfortable and memorable as possible — true Nepali hospitality at its finest.

Hills & Landscapes

Enjoy stunning views of Ghachowk, Lwang and Ghalel hills and their scenic landscapes — all beautifully visible right from the hotel.
